Privacy Policy for HomeScan

Effective Date: November 12, 2025

1. Introduction

HomeScan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the HomeScan mobile application (the "App"). The App provides property intelligence and information services for properties in Northern Ireland.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Permission

HomeScan requests camera permission to allow you to:

  • Scan property documents or QR codes
  • Capture photos of properties for your personal reference

Important: Photos taken using the camera are processed locally on your device and are not automatically uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to save or share them through the App.

2.2 Location Data

We may collect location information to:

  • Provide property-specific information based on your search area
  • Display relevant properties near you
  • Improve the accuracy of property searches

2.3 Property Search Data

We collect information about properties you search for, including:

  • Property addresses and postcodes
  • Search queries and filters
  • Reports generated

2.4 Device Information

We may collect technical information about your device, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and settings
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide property intelligence reports and information
  • Process your requests for property data
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We access public data from the following official sources:

  • Police Service of Northern Ireland (crime statistics)
  • Department of Education Northern Ireland (school information)
  • Planning Portal Northern Ireland (planning applications)
  • Ofcom (broadband coverage data)
  • Other publicly available government databases

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the App (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication processes
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data by employees

Your data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke camera or location permissions at any time through your device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

HomeScan is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a notification (where applicable)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you grant camera or location permissions
  • Contract: To provide the services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our App and ensure security
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the UK/EEA. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with GDPR requirements.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@homescan.ie
Company: HomeScan Limited
Address: Northern Ireland, United Kingdom
